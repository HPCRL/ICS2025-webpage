--- layout: home background: '/img/bg-index.jpg' --- # Important dates for paper submission ICS'2025 has two paper submission cycles ** Cycle 1 ** - Abstract submission: January 13, 2025 - Paper submission: January 16, 2025 - Rebuttal period: February 19 to February 21, 2025 - Author notification: March 14, 2025 - Camera-ready: April 14, 2025 ** Cycle 2 ** - Abstract submission: February 24, 2025 - Paper submission: February 27, 2025 - Rebuttal period: March 26 to March 28, 2025 - Author notification: April 10, 2025
